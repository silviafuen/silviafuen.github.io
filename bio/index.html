Who am I

I prefer to wear multiple hats in the product process plus watch some cartoons.

I spend my time helping plan, develop and launch user-focused digital products. I've had a chance to see and understand that planning is highly important in producing good, stable products. From designing and handling, documentation of a Travel site, to working on methods to allow multiple user cases in a library system. I've learned to approach design in a way that's more based on fluid possibilities and growth options.

Along with focus' in Marketing, Math, and Spanish. I've had a chance to take on and learn thoroughly the things that were daunting to me, like my native language, or Taylor series. In taking on these other areas, I think it's helped me have a better idea of how I want to see and show others the world.

Aside from designing on a computer, I like interior design and making sure everything around me is calm, except my closet, it's a hodgepodge.
