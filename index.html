Developer with a heart for Design.

What I do

Graphic Design

I am not the best paper and pen artist, but I do like to start from there drafting. I like to design with less and keep my works simple but never too minimalistic. I have worked a lot in creating invitations, social media graphics, and various icons sets.

User Interface & Experience

I have sworn off some sites because of how cluttered their layout is. I believe an interface should flow and make sense, not just deliver attractive visuals but ones with purpose. I like to work focusing on the analytics first and designing the frameworks from there.

Front End Development

I started learning HTML, and CSS, bits of javascript to better my Tumblr theme in high school. From there I saw there was a possibility to make meaningful stuff better. I enjoy developing interfaces and the logic behind them as well. I believe the planning process is essential, but so is making adjustments to a plan when necessary.

Product Concepting

I tend to go off on tangents with an idea, but that helps me visualize how a product can work across multiple platforms. I enjoy imagining how far something can reach before producing it. Though I am one to stay away from Trends because growth is great, but it's much better to develop something long lasting and personal to a user. That is what keeps a product going.
