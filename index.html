Developer with a heart for Design.

What I do

Graphic Design

I'm not the best paper and pen artist, but I do like to start from there drafting. I like to design with less and keep my works simple but never too miniumlistic. I've worked a lot in designing invitations, social media sharables and various icons sets.

User Interface & Experience

I've sworn off a number of sites because of how cluttered their layout is. I believe that an interface should flow and make sense, not just deliver nice visuals but visuals with a purpose, and not overload a user with content to they click away. I like to work focusing on the analytics first and then designing a frame from there.

Front End Development

I started learning html, and CSS, bits of javascript to better my tumblr theme in high school. From there I saw there was a possiblity to make a lot of stuff better. I enjoy developing interfaces and the logic behind them as well. Though I've learned to be all about planning and thinking through every small piece before I even touch a keyboard.

Product Concepting

I can go off on tangents with an idea and it helps me vision how it can work across multiple platforms and imagining where a poduct can be extended to. I can say one to stray away from trends because, growth is great but it's better to developing something long lasting and personal to a user. That's what keeps a product going.
