Coaches Caravan

Graphic Design, Event Branding, Marketing

Working along with the Atheletics divison of Tarleton State, I designed visuals to be used during their campaign to promote the first annual Coaches caravan event. This event differed from ones they usually promote on as they were approaching a wide auidence of sports fans, current students, potentional students and alumni.

Having that in mind I created 5 key elements using the Caravan play on words and giving a hint at the sports involved. I designed supporting elements for email and sharing on facebook, twitter and instagram.
