Though I wasn't at the time of the project able to see it to completion I recently decided to come back to this project to complete it the way I had intended and as well help me learn how to better develop email templates. - Tarleton State University Athletics Department - Develop a graphic for combined audience event in a variety of dimensions, while keeping style. - Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop, HTML/CSS to create email template. ---

Graphics

These graphics ranged from vertical, square, horizontal and email heading. I kept the important visual pieces of the design/event which were the University logo and the sports. I also tried to make the font strong because of sports, but still fun to a degree

Email

I had a lot more liberty here with the email, but decided to keep it instyle with similar heading font, and basic body text. Take a look at the email template.
